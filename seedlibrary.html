Little Elm Seed Library

Enjoy the great outdoors, help create a more sustainable world and learn useful new skills! Check out some seeds from our Library and start growing today!

Our Seed Library is one of our latest new services and is located in the east corner of the library next to the Printing Station.

Using the Seed Library

Patrons can browse the seed packets (which include flowers, herbs, fruits and vegetables) or they can consult the index on the table next to the seeds. Up to eight packets of seed can be checked out per library card.

After making their selection, the seeds are checked out at the circulation desk for a period of six months. This way, patrons can grow the plants, harvest them and return new seeds for other patrons to enjoy. However, in the case of crop failure, returning the seeds is not mandatory.

Additionally, if you need further information on growing your plants, the library has numerous books on gardening and landscaping. If you have a particular book that you would like to request or a certain plant that you would like seeds for, please let us know.
