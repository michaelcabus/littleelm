Children's Resources

  • Tumble Books!
  • Tumble Book Library is a collection of online e-books for children up to 12 years old. There are over 200 animated, talking picture books with exciting audio narration, highlighted text, and engaging illustrations. There are also 93 language learning books, 25 Read-Alongs, and 22 non fiction books.
    • Access
  • Rocket Languages
  • Rocket Languages is an award-winning interactive online language learning system. The multi-faceted, comprehensive system allows you to learn conversational language at your own pace. The DRM-free program allows you to fully utilize Rocket Language's easy to access downloadable lessons, games and more. YOU continue to access the program until you determine you have completed the course.
  • Available Languages: Spanish (Latin American); Arabic; French; German; Mandarin Chinese; Hindi; Italian; Japanese; Korean; American Sign Language; English for Spanish Speakers
