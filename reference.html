Interlibrary Loan

If you need a journal article, book or other loanable material that the Little Elm Public Library does not own, the Library's Interlibrary Loan (ILL) service will attempt to get it for you. You may request or have active two items at any one time.

  • Interlibrary Loan Request

Tips for Using Interlibrary Loan

  • When searching for an item, you will see a list of libraries owning the item. This is information for you to consult, but you do not need to pick one of these libraries.
  • The ILL system will make the choice based on availability. If the Little Elm Public Library System owns the desired item, you cannot request it through Interlibrary Loan, but you can search the library catalog and place a hold there.
  • Under "Find a copy in the Library," you do not need to choose one of the libraries that own the item. The system with pick for you.
  • If you do not have an email address, type "none." The preferred method of contact is email. However, if you do not have an email address, staff will contact you by phone.

Library Services

Whether personal or professional, we offer services to help keep you connected, including free Wi-Fi, staff-assisted faxing, copying and scanning, and 30 public computers.

Public Computers

Public computers have black and white/color printing capability, and we also offer large-format/poster printing and 3D printing. Please email reference@littleelmlibrary.org or call 214-975-0430 for more info.

Meeting Spaces

The library offers convenient meeting rooms by appointment for individual use or to small groups and non-profit organizations. Equipment includes:

  • flat panel display screen,
  • an interactive whiteboard projector,
  • webcam,
  • and microphone.

We also have two small meeting rooms which may be checked out via library card on a first-come-first-served basis.

If you have any questions, please email reference@littleelmlibrary.org or call 214-975-0430.
